About Us

Empowering Aspiring Seafarers Across India

The Global Cadet Ship Program is a welfare initiative established by the Global Seafarers Organization with the noble objective of guiding and supporting unemployed youth who aspire to build a successful career at sea in the Merchant Navy industry.

Our mission is to provide genuine, transparent, and structured guidance for students and job seekers who wish to join the Merchant Navy — completely free of charge.

  • Supported by Seafarers' Welfare Organizations & Trade Unions
  • Backed by reputed Shipping Companies and Maritime Institutes
  • Step-by-step career roadmaps for Deck, Engine, ETO & GP Rating
  • Sponsorship guidance and educational loan information
  • Admission assistance through certified career counsellors

Career Paths

Merchant Navy Departments

Choose your department and begin your maritime career journey. Each department offers a unique career path with global opportunities.

Deck Department

Navigate the world's oceans as a Deck Officer. Responsible for ship navigation, voyage planning, cargo operations, and bridge watchkeeping. Progress from Deck Cadet to Captain.

Engine Department

Manage propulsion engines, auxiliary machinery, boilers, and all technical systems onboard. Advance from Junior Engineer to Chief Engineer.

ETO – Electro Technical Officer

Oversee electrical systems, automation, navigation electronics, and high-voltage equipment. One of the most in-demand technical roles on modern vessels.

GP Ratings

Assist both Deck and Engine departments. Perform maintenance, watchkeeping, mooring, and cargo assistance. A great entry point to a maritime career.

Saloon Department

Manage catering, food preparation, crew accommodation, laundry, and hospitality services onboard merchant vessels.

Marine Fitters & Welders

Skilled technical personnel handling fabrication, welding, pipe repairs, and structural maintenance. Vital for ship maintenance operations.
