Diploma in Nautical Science (DNS) – 1 Year Programme

Approved & Career-Oriented Maritime Training

Approved by the Directorate General of Shipping (DGS), Mumbai, Government of India

Offered under Technical Collaboration with Alagappa University, Tamil Nadu

1-Year Full-Time Residential Programme

Pathway to Obtain B.Sc. Nautical Science Degree

The Diploma in Nautical Science (DNS) Programme is designed for students aspiring to build a successful career as Deck Officers in the Merchant Navy and progress towards becoming a Captain / Master Mariner.

The programme combines academic learning, practical maritime training, navigation knowledge, seamanship, cargo handling, ship operations, and safety procedures in accordance with international maritime standards. The one-year course consists of two semesters, and examinations are conducted by Alagappa University, Tamil Nadu.

Upon successful completion of the programme, cadets are awarded the Diploma in Nautical Science and become eligible to join merchant vessels as Trainee Deck Cadets. During onboard training, cadets gain valuable sea experience and practical exposure essential for professional competency development.

After completion of the required sea service and competency training, candidates become eligible to appear for the Second Mate Examination, leading to promotion as Second Officer. With additional sea experience, competency examinations, and operational expertise, officers can steadily progress to Chief Officer and eventually become Captain / Master Mariner.

The DNS Programme also provides an opportunity to obtain a B.Sc. Nautical Science Degree through the Distance Learning Programme (DLP) conducted by Alagappa University during the sea training period. Guidance and placement assistance are provided to deserving and meritorious students for building a successful maritime career.
