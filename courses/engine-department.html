Engine Department

The Engine Department manages:

  • Main propulsion engines
  • Auxiliary machinery
  • Boilers and pumps
  • Engine room systems
  • Fuel systems
  • Refrigeration systems
  • Power generation equipment
  • Ship machinery maintenance

Marine Engineers ensure smooth and safe operation of all mechanical and technical systems onboard.
