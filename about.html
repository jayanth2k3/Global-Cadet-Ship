Who We Are

About Global Cadet Ship Program

The Global Cadet Ship Program is a welfare initiative established by the Global Seafarers Organization with the noble objective of guiding and supporting unemployed youth who aspire to build a successful career at sea in the Merchant Navy industry.

This initiative is supported by:

Seafarers' Welfare Organizations
Seafarers' Trade Unions
Shipping Companies
Reputed Maritime Training Institutes

Our mission is to provide genuine, transparent, and structured guidance for students and job seekers who wish to join the Merchant Navy.

Our Services

What We Provide

Global Cadet Ship Program offers complete information and assistance regarding:

Merchant Navy Career Opportunities

Comprehensive guidance on career opportunities across Deck, Engine, ETO, GP Rating, and Catering Departments in the Merchant Navy.

Eligibility & Qualification Requirements

Complete eligibility criteria and qualification requirements for all Merchant Navy courses and career paths.

DGS Approved Maritime Academies

Guidance on DGS Shipping approved maritime academies and training institutes throughout India.

Sponsorship Programs & Guidance

Information about shipping companies offering employment and sponsorship opportunities for qualified cadets.

Educational Loan & Bank Loan Information

Assistance with information regarding educational loans and bank loan options available for maritime training programmes.

Admission Assistance

Admission assistance through certified career counsellors for reputed government-approved maritime training institutes.

Career Support

Student Guidance & Career Support

Our experienced and certified counsellors provide professional support to interested candidates in selecting suitable courses and securing admission in reputed government-approved maritime institutes.
